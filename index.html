About Us

BECA+LOAF is a project I started on my own after the pandemic. It is a way for me to express myself through fashion but also give back to the community.This is just the Front Page that showcases the newest work. Please click on the Main Page Link in the Navigation Bar
BECA+LOAF aims to provide products that remain timeless and compete against the fast-fashion market with the quality and design that speaks for itself. We aim for personalization for the customer with high-caliber designs that last the swing of the season and thus remain timeless in their endeavors.
